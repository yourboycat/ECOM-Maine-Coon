Parents

Meet our beloved pair of thoroughbred Main Coone adult cats, Ch Akella Leader Yasmin of Dark Moon (IMP) & Dark Moon Kali, the proud parents of the adorable kittens currently available for adoption. Yasmin, with her majestic long fur and striking amber eyes, exudes grace and elegance. She is known for her affectionate nature and enjoys lounging in the sunlight streaming through the windows. Kali, on the other hand, is a gentle giant with a regal demeanor and a magnificent tufted mane. His playful spirit and outgoing personality make him a favorite among visitors. Together, Ch Akella Leader Yasmin of Dark Moon (IMP) & Dark Moon Kali epitomize the beauty and charm of the Main Coone breed, passing down their exceptional traits to their offspring. As responsible breeders, we ensure that all our kittens receive the utmost care and love from their first moments, ensuring they grow into healthy, well-socialized companions.
